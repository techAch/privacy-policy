Refund Policy

RiderMax Manager — Premium subscriptions

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

This Refund Policy applies to RiderMax Manager premium subscriptions (Monthly member and Yearly member). Payments are processed by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record. Paddle handles checkout, tax, invoicing, and payment disputes on our behalf.

Digital product: Premium is a digital subscription with immediate access after payment. There is no physical product to return.

14-day refund window

If you are not satisfied with Premium, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase or renewal charge, provided you have not extensively used premium features in bad faith (e.g. bulk export of data solely to obtain a refund).

After 14 days, subscription fees are generally non-refundable for the current billing period. You will retain access until the end of the period you already paid for.

Cancellations

You may cancel auto-renewal at any time. Cancellation stops future charges but does not automatically refund the current period unless eligible under the 14-day rule above.

  • Monthly member (11.99 MAD/month): access until the end of the paid month.
  • Yearly member (124.99 MAD/year): access until the end of the paid year.

To cancel, use the subscription management link in your Paddle receipt email, or contact us at the address below with your order email and purchase date.

How to request a refund

  1. Email the.greate.game@gmail.com with subject “Refund request”.
  2. Include the email used at checkout, approximate purchase date, and plan (monthly or yearly).
  3. If you have a Paddle order or receipt number, include it — this speeds up processing.

We aim to respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed by Paddle to your original payment method. Bank processing may take an additional 5–10 business days depending on your provider.

Chargebacks

Please contact us before opening a chargeback with your bank. We will work in good faith to resolve billing issues. Unjustified chargebacks may result in loss of premium access and inability to purchase future subscriptions.

Failed or duplicate charges

If you were charged twice or charged after cancelling renewal, contact us with proof of payment. Verified duplicate or erroneous charges will be refunded in full.

Changes to premium or pricing

We may update features or prices for future billing periods. Price changes do not affect your current paid period. See our Pricing page for current plans.

Consumer rights

Nothing in this policy limits mandatory rights you may have under applicable consumer protection laws in your country of residence.
