Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this application handles user data, including account information when you sign in with Google.

Data Collection

When you sign in with Google, the application uses Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In to authenticate your account. This may involve collecting and processing your Google account profile information, such as your name, email address, and a unique user identifier, along with authentication-related information needed to keep you signed in.

Login information is stored securely through Firebase Authentication so the app can recognize your account and maintain your sign-in session.

Local Storage

Trip history, expenses, calculations, and app settings are stored locally on the user's device only.

Account login information is managed through Firebase Authentication rather than being stored only on the device.

Analytics

This application doesn't use any analytics services. No data is sent to third-party analytics providers.

Third-Party Services

This application uses Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication to provide account access. Google and Firebase may receive the information required to authenticate your account and provide these services.

Offline Usage

The application is designed to function offline for core app features, although account sign-in and syncing may require an internet connection.

Data Deletion

Users may remove locally stored data by clearing the application data or uninstalling the application. To request deletion of account-related data managed through Firebase, please contact the developer.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer.
