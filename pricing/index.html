What we sell

RiderMax Manager (package com.couriercalc.pro) is a delivery earnings calculator and route planner for couriers. The app is free to use with core features. Premium is an optional digital subscription that unlocks advanced tools for tracking earnings, analytics, and customization.

Subscriptions are sold as recurring digital services. There is no physical shipment. Premium access is linked to your purchase account and applied in the app after payment is confirmed.

Premium includes

  • Full earnings breakdown and profit analysis
  • Dark mode and theme customization
  • Delivery reminders and notifications
  • Automatic traffic adjustments for time estimates
  • Daily earnings goal tracking
  • Advanced history filters and date ranges
  • Extended delivery history (unlimited records)
  • Charts, heatmaps, and performance analytics
  • Custom location presets
  • Export and import data (JSON / CSV)
  • Custom rate editing in delivery records

Free tier

Without a subscription, you can still use core delivery estimation, save a limited history, and access essential settings. Premium features show a lock icon until you subscribe.

How to purchase

Subscriptions are purchased through our authorized payment partner Paddle.com (Merchant of Record). Checkout may open in your browser from the RiderMax Manager app or website. After a successful payment, premium access is activated on the account or device associated with your purchase.

Billing & cancellation

  • Monthly plans renew every month on the anniversary of your purchase date.
  • Yearly plans renew every 12 months on the anniversary of your purchase date.
  • You can cancel renewal at any time; access continues until the end of the paid period.
  • Manage or cancel via the link in your Paddle receipt email or contact us below.
